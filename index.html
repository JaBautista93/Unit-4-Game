Gummy Bear Rebellion!

Gummy Bears have offically rebelled and are running rampant, in order to survie you have to play the guessing game with the 4 Gummy Horsemen. Stop them before their plans of Dibates for all humans becomes a reality

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four Gummy Bears below. By clicking on the Gummy Bear you will add a specific amount of Gummy Points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total Gummy score to random Gummy score, you lose the game if your total score goes aboe the random Gummy score.

The value of each Gummy Bear is hidden from you until you clock on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each Gummy Bear
